Got Questions?

What size are the plates? +

Our standard plate is approximately 52cm × 11cm — the same size as a real Indian number plate. Custom sizes are available at an additional charge.

Can I put any text on my plate? +

Yes! Any letters, numbers, names, initials, words, or short phrases. Standard price covers up to 8 characters including spaces. Add-on charges apply for more.

What material is the plate made from? +

Our plates are made from high-quality acrylic with UV-resistant print finish — durable, lightweight, and perfect for indoor display.

How do I place an order? +

Just click the WhatsApp button on this page! Tell us your text, style, and delivery address. We'll confirm the price and guide you through payment — all on WhatsApp.

Do you take bulk or gifting orders? +

Absolutely — plates make amazing personalised gifts! Message us for bulk pricing and we'll work something out for you.
